## About this App
<!--  -->
SpaceFM Romania is your gateway to an auditory voyage through time and rhythm. Immerse yourself in the captivating world of online radio, where you can relish the melodic offerings of not one, but two distinct channels: SpaceFM Dance and SpaceFM Retro. Tune in to SpaceFM Dance for a pulsating journey through contemporary beats that'll make your feet move, or switch over to SpaceFM Retro for a nostalgic retreat into the timeless classics of yesteryears. With SpaceFM Romania, your auditory senses will traverse the spectrum of music, ensuring an unforgettable listening experience that transcends eras and genres.
